Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, update your profile, or communicate with other users. This includes:

  • Name and email address
  • Parish affiliation
  • Leadership status (A-Squared membership)
  • Profile information and bio
  • Posts, comments, and messages you create

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Connect you with your parish community
  • Send you notifications about fellowship activities
  • Protect against fraud and abuse

3. Information Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties except:

  • With your consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect the rights and safety of our users

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account
  • Opt out of communications

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@ayfgwarimpa.org
